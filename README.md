# Cirqly 

Cirqly is a public research and experimentation space exploring creator networks, collaborative growth, and alternative models to traditional follower-based platforms.

## What this is

A collection of concepts, visual experiments, and early explorations around how creators can grow through connection rather than competition.

## Focus areas

- creator network structures
- collaborative discovery systems
- audience overlap and reciprocity
- visual representations of digital ecosystems
- cooperative growth models

## Philosophy

Most social platforms are built around individual accumulation.

Cirqly explores what happens when growth becomes **network-driven** instead.

## Notes

This repository contains public-facing concepts and exploratory work only.  
It does not include proprietary product logic or private implementation details.
